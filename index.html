Modal.js

data-modal

Hi! I'm a Modal

You can close me by clicking anywhere in the page but myself!

All you have to do is to include modal.js and add data-modal attribute to your modal AND elements which activate the modal.

Modal.js will remove modalClass || 'active' class from the modal when clicked away.

It's not only about Modals, you can use Modal.js to provide click-away-to-close functionality to any element.
